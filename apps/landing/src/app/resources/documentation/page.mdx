[Pipe0](https://pipe0.com) is a framework for data enrichment. It replaces providers 
like Zoominfo, Prospeo, or Findymail with a more flexible API.

We're developing three endpoints that allow everyone to integrate features similar to [n8n](https://n8n.io) (node-based automation)
or [clay](https://clay.com) (table-based automation) into their applications at a fair price.

**Pipes:** A composable endpoint to enrich data & perform actions in 3rd party systems <br />
**Searches:**: A composable endpoint to search for companies and leads using filters <br />
**Comming soon:** A data transforamtion endpoint <br />

Our endpoints work great as a team. However, you can use each endpoint in isolation too.

Aside from finding people, companies, email addresses, phone numbers, LinkedIn profiles
(s. <AppLink linkType="pipeCatalog">pipe catalog</AppLink> & <AppLink linkType="searchCatalog">search catalog</AppLink> for a full list) and more,
you can use Pipe0 to express conditions, perform actions, and generate insights with AI.

It's easy to underestimate the engineering challenge of building a robust data enrichment platform in-house.
